> Privacy

Last updated 2026-05-08.

What we collect from you

Nothing on the site itself. There are no signups, accounts, forms, comments, or submission flows. Browsing is read-only.

If you accept analytics in the cookie banner, Google Analytics records a pseudonymous visit (anonymized IP, country, page path, referrer, browser). We don't combine this with anything else to identify you, and we don't sell or share it.

Cookies and local storage

  • _ga, _ga_* — Google Analytics. Only set after you click Accept in the cookie banner. Retained 24 months. Stores a pseudonymous visitor ID.
  • cookieConsent (localStorage) — your accept/decline choice so we don't keep nagging you. Never sent to any third party.
  • aggregatorHide (localStorage) — your "Hide aggregator reposts" toggle state. Stays on this device.

Decline analytics → no cookies are set; the banner remembers your choice in localStorage only.

Third parties that may see your IP

The site is static, but loading it touches a few CDNs and APIs. They see your IP and User-Agent as part of normal HTTP traffic:

  • Vercel — hosts the site.
  • Google Analytics — only after you accept analytics.
  • Google Fonts — serves the Fira Code / Fira Sans webfonts.
  • jsDelivr CDN — serves AG Grid and the Supabase JS client.
  • Supabase — read-only fallback when the static positions snapshot can't load. Anonymous public reads only.

What we do with positions

Each position links back to the original Bluesky post. The post text and author handle are public on Bluesky; we re-display them with a link to the source. The author always controls the original.

To remove your own Bluesky post from this aggregator, email eli.eydlin@gmail.com. We'll delete it from our database within a few days.

Your rights

  • Opt out of analytics: click Decline in the cookie banner, or install the Google Analytics Opt-out add-on. Already accepted? Clear cookieConsent from localStorage and reload — the banner reappears so you can switch.
  • EU/UK visitors: our default-deny consent state means no analytics cookies are set unless you opt in. You can request access, correction, or deletion of any data we hold via the contact email below.

Contact

Questions, removal requests, or anything privacy-related: eli.eydlin@gmail.com.
